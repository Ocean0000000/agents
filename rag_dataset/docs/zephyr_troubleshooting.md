# Zephyr Air Purifier Troubleshooting

## Common Issues
### Unit will not power on
- Check the power cable is fully inserted.
- Confirm the outlet has power.
- Replace the fuse if the display is blank.

### Weak airflow
- Clean or replace the pre-filter.
- Ensure the rear intake is not blocked.
- Replace the HEPA filter every 6 months.

### Loud vibration
- Make sure the unit is on a level surface.
- Check that the filter cover is seated correctly.

## Reset Filter Light
Hold the Power button for 5 seconds while the unit is on.
