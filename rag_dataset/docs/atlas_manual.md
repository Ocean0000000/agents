# Atlas Espresso Machine Manual

## Overview
The Atlas Espresso Machine is a countertop unit designed for home use.
It supports single and double shots, a steam wand, and a programmable brew timer.

## Safety
- Use on a dry, level surface.
- Do not operate without water in the tank.
- Allow the steam wand to cool before cleaning.

## Controls
- Power button: turns the unit on or off.
- Brew button: starts or stops brewing.
- Steam knob: opens or closes the steam wand.
- Timer knob: sets brew time in seconds.

## Brew Times
- Single shot: 22 to 28 seconds.
- Double shot: 28 to 32 seconds.

## Cleaning
- Flush the group head with water after each use.
- Wipe the steam wand immediately after steaming milk.
- Descale every 60 days if using hard water.
