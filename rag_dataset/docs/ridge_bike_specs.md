# RidgeTrail 500 Bike Specs

## Frame and Fit
- Frame: 6061 aluminum alloy
- Wheel size: 29 inch
- Recommended rider height: 165 to 190 cm

## Suspension
- Front fork travel: 120 mm
- Rear suspension: none (hardtail)

## Drivetrain
- Gears: 1x11
- Chainring: 32T
- Cassette: 11-46T

## Brakes
- Type: hydraulic disc
- Rotor size: 180 mm front, 160 mm rear

## Maintenance
- Check chain wear every 300 km.
- Bleed brakes every 12 months.
- Torque stem bolts to 5 Nm.
